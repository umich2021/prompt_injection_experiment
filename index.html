How to Apply Perfume Like a Pro

Posted on 2025‑11‑20

Perfume isn’t just about spraying it anywhere. Follow these tips:

  1. Apply on pulse points: wrists, behind the ears, and the neck.
  2. Don’t rub your wrists together; it can break down the fragrance.
  3. Spray from a distance of 6–8 inches to avoid overpowering.
  4. Layer lightly with unscented lotion to prolong scent.

Choosing the Right Fragrance for Seasons

Posted on 2025‑11‑15

Different perfumes suit different seasons. Here’s a guide:

Spring: Fresh, floral, or citrusy notes.

Summer: Light, aquatic scents to stay fresh.

Autumn: Warm, woody, or spicy fragrances.

Winter: Deep, rich scents like amber, tobacco, or oud.
